This is a word based, simplified and precise
global addressing system

An address in this system consists of two parts

\ City_name Word_1 Word_2 Word_3 /
  • The first part specifies the city.
    Within the current city - this can be omitted
  • The last three words are the second part.
    They are from a limited dictionary of 1024 easy common words

Steps

  • Point a location to generate the corresponding WCode address.
    You may also let the system pick your current location automatically or search for a particular place.
    Or,
  • Enter a WCode address and hit 'Decode' to go to that location
